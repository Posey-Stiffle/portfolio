About Me

Ray Palomino

Hey there! I'm studying for my Digital Media Arts (DMA) BFA. I'm half-Japanese, half-American and traveled across the globe, I was born in Japan and lived in 4 different states in the U.S.A. This experience being around the world allows me look at art using different perspectives and world views.

I'm passionate about story telling and I'm always trying to find a way to incoporate stories into my work. Be it photography, animation or illustration.
