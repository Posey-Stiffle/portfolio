Instead of utilizing stamps as present in the examples, I opted to make my program have brushes as its drawing tools. The user can choose from 3 different brush sizes by click on the number keys 1, 2, and 4. 1 is the smallest brush and 4 is the biggest. The number 5 key changes the color of the brush, number 6 generates a series of translucent squares, 7 is a spray tool and 8 is a softer brush that's slightly transparent, 9 changes the background color, 0 is an oval brush and finally the - key is an eraser brush. With my drawing program I re-drew a screenshot from Family Guy where a horse enters the Family Guy residence and Lois says "Peter, the horse is here." In my version only the characters are present in the image and the horse is a character from hit game and show Umamusume. Overall, this project was fun and interesting, and I enjoyed it.
